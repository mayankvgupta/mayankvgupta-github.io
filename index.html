How I Work — Methodology
Every project follows the same four-phase loop — expanding outward to explore, then narrowing to act.
01
Discover
I start by listening — to users, stakeholders, and data — before forming any opinions about the solution.
User interviews Stakeholder sessions Competitive audit Analytics review
02
Define
I synthesize findings into a clear problem statement — so the team agrees on what we're solving before how.
Synthesis + themes Personas + JTBD Problem statement Success metrics
03
Design
I explore many directions quickly, test with real users, and iterate until the evidence supports a direction.
Lo-fi sketches Prototyping Usability testing Hi-fi iteration
04
Deliver
I hand off with full specs, stay close through build, and measure outcomes to inform the next cycle.
Eng handoff + spec QA + polish Launch + rollout Metrics review ↺

About

I work best at the intersection of research, systems, and storytelling.

Hi, I'm Mayank — a veteran Front End Developer turned User Experience Designer and Researcher based in Santa Clara with over 6 years of design experience. I specialize in analyzing user behaviors through qualitative and quantitative research and creating sustainable user flows for all stakeholders.

As an accessibility advocate, I strongly believe that everyone should have the same experience while using digital products. That belief shapes every decision I make, from information architecture down to interaction details.

Outside work

After work, you can find me in the kitchen experimenting with new recipes or on the couch playing open world video games. There is no in between!
