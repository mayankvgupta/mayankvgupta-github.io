Home / About Me

Hi, I’m Mayank. I think creativity is not just about creating beautiful products but about creating sustainable experiences. Every product, no matter how small, provides the user an experience. I’m intrigued by this philosophy and determined to enhance every user’s digital experience.

While pursuing my masters in Information Experience Design at Pratt Institute, I redesigned the Placemaking Week website’s user experience. I helped improve the experience for Barnard’s Digital Collections. I co-led the design of a Skill-Sharing Application, for the beta space at Pratt.

Besides all of this, I am fond of reading and cooking. I also love travelling, photography, sketching, lettering and listening to music (I am a drummer).

I’m based in New York, but originally hail from New Delhi, India. You can join me anytime for the best Indian food in town.

Thank you! Feel free to connect with opportunities.
