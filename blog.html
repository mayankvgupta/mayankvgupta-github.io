Design Critique: Splitwise (Android App)

by Mayank Gupta
|
in UX Reasearch

Splitwise, a really popular application which was launched in 2011, helps people by making it easier to split bills and other expenses. It stores the user data on the cloud giving them the flexibility to access it anytime, anywhere. Splitwise provides a really aesthetic and clean UI to help people manage their expenses by giving them a clear picture of who owes whom along with the amount owed.
